Fun Facts about The Office

  • There are 4 actors that share first names with their characters, Angela Kinsley, Creed Bratton, Oscar Nunez and Phyllis Smith
  • There is one character who shares the same exact name, Creed Bratton
  • Mindy Kaling, BJ Novack and Paul Liberstien were not only characters but part of the original team of writers
  • The show was hilarious throughout all 9 Seasons, regardless of what anyone says
